keto Recipes for 2000-2200 Calories

Tossed okra

  • Tuna,4 oz
  • Sour cream,1/2 tbsp
  • Salsa, 2 tbsp
  • Avocados, 1/4 cup
Drain tuna and place into bowl.
Remove pit from avocado and scoop fruit into bowl with tuna.
Add salsa and sour cream to bowl. Mix everything together well and serve.
Calorie count: 200 cal

Plain Tuna Salad

  • Tuna,1 can
  • salt,1 dash
  • pepper,1 dash
  • Mayonnaise, 1 tbsp
Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
Serve with bread or side of choice if desired, just be sure to add that item to your plan so those calories are tracked! Enjoy! Calorie count: 388 cal

Balsamic Avocado snack

  • Balsamic vinegar, 1 tsp
  • Olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • Avocados, 1 fruit
    • Stir together oil and vinegar. Cut the avocado in half, and discard the pit; divide the sauce between the avocado halves. Eat with a spoon! Calorie count:480 cal

Cauliflower and Tahinia

  • Cauliflower,1 cup chopped
  • Sesame Tahini,2 tbsp
    • Chop the cauliflower lovingly, to retain some of the florets.
    Serve cauliflower pieces with tahini for dipping. Enjoy!
    Calorie count: 207
