Grilled Salmon with Roasted Asparagus:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 bunches asparagus
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Brush the salmon fillets and asparagus with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
Grill the salmon for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
Roast the asparagus in the oven at 400°F for 12-15 minutes, or until tender and slightly charred.
Serve the grilled salmon with the roasted asparagus.

Calorie count: 500 cal

Grilled Chicken with Asparagus

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 can black beans,
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Cook the quinoa according to package instructions.
In a separate bowl, combine the diced avocado and black beans.
When the quinoa is cooked, add it to the bowl with the avocado and black beans. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
Calorie count: 450 cal

Mediterranean-style Tuna Salad

  • 2 cans tuna, drained
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Combine the tuna, red onion, Kalamata olives, and fresh parsley in a mixing bowl.
    Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
    Stir to combine.
    Serve immediately or store in the refrigerator for later. Calorie count: 500 cal
