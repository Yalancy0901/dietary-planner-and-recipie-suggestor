Vegetarian Recipes for 1800-2000 Calories

Evergreen Smoothie

  • 1 cup, of baby spinach
  • Curd, ½ cup
  • Mint leaves, a handful
  • Rock salt, a pinch
  • Jeera powder, ¼ tsp
  • Amla, a piece, or lemon juice, ½ tsp
And blend !!!!!
Calorie count: 95 cal

Nutty-delight Smoothie

  • Puffed amaranth/rajgira,½ cup

  • Banana, ½ and date,1
  • melon seeds, 1 tsp
  • Flax seeds, 1 tsp
  • Chia seeds, 1 tsp
And blend !!!!!
Calorie count: 295 cal

Zucchini "Pasta"

  • Pasta sauce, 1/2 cup
  • Spaghetti/marinara, ready-to-serve
  • Zucchini,1 medium
  • Parmesan cheese,1/3 cup
    • Peel Zucchini with Peeler to form Ribbons.
    Add Sauce and Cheese.
    Either heat up in microwave or on stovetop in medium skillet.
    Calorie count: 207
