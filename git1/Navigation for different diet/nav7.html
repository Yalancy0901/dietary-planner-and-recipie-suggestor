Vegan Recipes for 2200-2500 Calories

Japanese soba

  • Japanese noodles,2 oz
  • Coconut milk,1/4 cup
  • Curry paste,1/2 cup
  • Almond butter,3/4 tbsp
Bring water to boil in a large saucepot and briefly boil noodles
Drain noodles into colander, then return pot to burner
Stir or Whisk together almond butter, curry paste, coconut milk
once even consistency is reached, pour over noodles and serve immediately
Calorie count: 260 cal

Maple, Strawberry, and Banana Shake

  • Water,1 cup
  • Banana,1 medium
  • Strawberries,5 large
  • Maple syrups, 1 tbsp
  • Rolled oats,2 tbsp
Combine all ingredients in a blender and pulse until combined. Enjoy!
Calorie count: 220 cal

Endive salad

  • Balsamic vinegar, 1 tsp
  • Salt,1/2 tsp
  • Olive oil, 1 tbsp
  • Apples, 1 small
  • Endive,5 cup, chopped
    • Chop apple and endive. Toss all ingredients together well and serve. Enjoy!
    Calorie count: 240 cal
