Lemon Steamed Broccoli

  • Broccoli,3/4 ld
  • salt,1/2 tsp
  • pepper,1/2 tsp
  • Olive oil,1/2 tsp
  • Lemon juice,1/4 tbsp
Trim the broccoli into large florets.Place the broccoli in a steaming basket over boiling water; cover and steam for 3 minutes.
Remove the lid for a moment, then cook, partially covered, until the stems are tender-firm, another 8-10 minutes.
Remove to a platter; season with salt and pepper, olive oil, and the lemon juice. Calorie count: 180 cal
