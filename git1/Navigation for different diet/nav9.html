Grilled Chicken with Roasted Vegetables

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 4 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Brush the chicken breasts and mixed vegetables with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
Roast the mixed vegetables in the oven at 400°F for 20-25 minutes, or until tender and slightly charred.
Serve the grilled chicken with the roasted vegetables.
Calorie count: 450 cal

Grilled Chicken with Asparagus

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 can black beans,
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Cook the quinoa according to package instructions.
In a separate bowl, combine the diced avocado and black beans.
When the quinoa is cooked, add it to the bowl with the avocado and black beans. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper to taste.

Sautéed Shrimp with Spinach and Tomatoes

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    Add the shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until pink and cooked through.
    Add the baby spinach and cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook for 1-2 minutes, or until the spinach is wilted and the tomatoes are slightly softened.
    Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    Calorie count: 300
