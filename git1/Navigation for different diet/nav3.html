Yogurt with Beets, Orange, & Dill

  • Nonfat greek yogurt
  • Beets, 1 cup, shredded
  • Oranges, 1 fruit
  • Dill weed, 2 tsp
Chop orange into segments.
Top yogurt with shredded beets, orange slices, and dill. Enjoy!
Calorie count: 395 cal

South-Indian Sambar

  • Onions,3/16 large
  • Curry powder,Spices,2/3 tbsp
  • Water,1 cup
  • Fenugreek seed,1/3 tsp
  • Coriander seed,2/3 tsp
  • Pumpkin,2/3 cup
  • Mustard seed,1/3 tsp
  • Jalapeno peppers,2/3 pepper
  • Tamarind nectar,2/3 tbsp
  • Pigeon peas,1/3 cup
  • White rice
    • Chop the tomatoes and onion.
