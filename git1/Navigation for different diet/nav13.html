Chilled Cucumber Avocado Soup

  • Cucumber,1 large
  • Chicken broth,2 cup
  • Garlic, 1 cloves
  • Avocados, 1/4 cup
  • Turmeric, 1 tsp
  • cilantro, 1/4 cup
Combine all ingredients in food processor or blender and blend to combine until consistency is uniform. Serve chilled and enjoy!
Calorie count: 445 cal

Provolone Lettuce Wrap

  • Roast beef,1 slice
  • Provolone cheese,2 oz
  • Red bell pepper,1/4 medium
  • Dijon mustard, 1 tbsp
  • Lettuce,1 leaf outer
Slice red bell pepper into 1” wide “sticks."
Lay out 1 large outter lettuce leaf. Fold beef in half and lay on lettuce leaf. Place provolone slice on top of beef. Calorie count: 231 cal

Cottage Cheese and Avocado

  • Cottage cheese, 1 cups
  • Cayenne pepper, 1 pinch
  • Avocados, 1 fruit
    • Slice avocado. Mix with cottage cheese and enjoy! Sprinkle with cayenne if desired. Spread mustard all over surface of cheese.Place 2 red bell pepper “sticks” in center. Fold up lettuce leaf to contain all the contents.
    Calorie count:485 cal
