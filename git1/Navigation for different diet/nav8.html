  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 sprigs fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Grilled Chicken with Asparagus

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 lb asparagus
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Preheat the grill to medium-high heat. Brush the chicken breasts and asparagus with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
Grill the asparagus for 4-6 minutes, or until tender and slightly charred.
Serve the chicken and asparagus together. Calorie count: 350 cal

Tofu Salad

  • 2 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Ice (optional)
    • Combine the bananas, peanut butter, almond milk, and honey (if using) in a blender.
