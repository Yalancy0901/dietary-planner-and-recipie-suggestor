Vegan Recipes for 1800-2000 Calories

Blackberry Almond Butter Sandwich

  • Whole-wheat bread,2 slice
  • Chia seeds,1/3 tbsp
  • Blackberries,1/2 cup
  • Almond butter,1 tbsp
Mash blackberries in a bowl and stir in chia seeds.
Toast bread and make the sandwich with almond butter and blackberry mixture, topping with other slice of bread.
Calorie count: 260 cal

Maple, Strawberry, and Banana Shake

  • Water,1 cup
  • Banana,1 medium
  • Strawberries,5 large
  • Maple syrups, 1 tbsp
  • Rolled oats,2 tbsp
Combine all ingredients in a blender and pulse until combined. Enjoy!
Calorie count: 220 cal
