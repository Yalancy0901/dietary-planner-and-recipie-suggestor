Vegan Recipes for 2000-2200 Calories

Banana Almond Butter

  • Banana,2 medium
  • Almond butter,2 tbsp
Mash banana. Combine with almond butter in a bowl and mix until smooth. Enjoy!
Calorie count: 350 cal

Easy Spinach and Scallion Salad

  • Spinach,5 cup
  • Scallions,2 large
  • Olive oil,1 tbsp
  • Lemon juice,1/2 lemon yields
  • Pepper, 1 dash
Wash spinach well, drain, and chop. Squeeze out excess water. Chop green onions.
Put spinach in a mixing bowl and add the scallions/green onions, oil, pepper, and the juice from 1 squeezed lemon. Toss and serve. Calorie count: 175 cal

Kale and Banana Smoothie

  • Almond milk, 1/2 cup
  • Kale,3 cup, chopped
  • Almond butter,1 tbsp
  • Banana,1 large
  • Vanilla extract,1/4 tsp
    • Combine all ingredients in a blender and pulse until smooth. Enjoy!
    Calorie count: 350 cal

Vegan Mexican Tortilla Roll

  • Tortillas,1 tortilla
  • Canned black beans, 1 can
  • Hummus,5/16 cup
  • Corn,1/4 cup kernels
  • Avocados,1/4 fruit,
  • onions,1/8 onion
  • Arugula,1/2 cup
  • Fresh cilantro,2 sprigs
Drain and rinse beans and corn. Chop veggies and cilantro.
Slather the tortillas with hummus, getting it all the way to the edges. Distribute all toppings amongst tortillas and roll tortillas up tightly.
Gently slice into bite-sized pieces and serve.
Calorie count: 400
