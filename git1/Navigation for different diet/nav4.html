Vegetarian Recipes for 2200-2500 Calories

Apple Toast

  • Whole-wheat bread,2 slice
  • Butter,1/2 tbsp
  • Cinnamon,1/2 tbsp
  • Apples,1/2 large
Set oven to broil.
Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Place apple slices on buttered side of bread. Sprinkle cinnamon on top. Place bread on a baking sheet.
Place in a preheated oven until toasted, about 2 minutes. Calorie count: 260 cal

Cheese On Toast

  • Multi-grain bread, 1 slice large
  • Cheddar cheese,1 slice
  • Butter,1 tsp
Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.Butter bread and place in a baking tray.
Bake for 3 minutes.Top with slices of cheese and completely cover bread.
Bake for another 5 minutes. Bring halves together to form a sandwich and enjoy! Calorie count: 250 cal

Avocado and Coconut smoothie

  • Avocados, 1 fruit
  • Vanilla yogurt, 1/4 cups
  • Reduced fat milk,1/4 cup
  • Coconut cream,2 tbsp
  • Ice cubes,4 cube
    • Combine avocado, yogurt, milk, cream of coconut,
    and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth.
    Calorie count: 360 cal

Black Bean Wrap

  • Tortillas,1 tortilla
  • Canned black beans, 1 can
  • Salsa,1/3 cup
  • Corn,1/4 cup kernels
  • Avocados,1/4 fruit,
  • Olives,1 tbsp
  • Arugula,1/2 cup
  • Fresh cilantro,2 sprigs
    • Mix black beans with the corn and salsa until well combined.
    Spoon filling onto the tortilla.
    Top with sliced avocado, olive, arugula, and cilantro. Roll up and enjoy! Calorie count: 650
